Capital Improvement Program

Definition

The Capital Improvement Program (CIP) includes, in general, new and continuing projects that are intended to construct or significantly extend the life of existing facilities, parks/open space, streets, sidewalks, and sewers; purchase technology related equipment and service contracts; mitigate traffic hazards; improve pedestrian and bicycle safety; and improve disabled persons’ access to City facilities. CIP projects generally cost more than $50,000.
